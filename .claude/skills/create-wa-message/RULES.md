## CORE CONCEPT

We pre-build a working AI assistant on the prospect's website BEFORE contacting them. Messages are sent as **video+caption** (single WhatsApp bubble). The text you write is the VIDEO CAPTION — it frames context for why they should watch the attached demo video.

**Three pillars:** (1) Signal-anchored personalization, (2) Pre-built demo as proof of effort, (3) Low-friction CTA.

---

## Rule 1: "Already Built For You" — Reciprocity Trigger

Every message MUST contain one reciprocity phrase:
- PT: "Ja montei/criei/preparei algo pro seu [negocio/site/WhatsApp]"
- EN: "I've already put together/set up/built something for your [business/site/WhatsApp]"
- ES: "Ya arme/prepare/cree algo para tu [negocio/sitio/WhatsApp]"

Never say "We offer", "Our product", "We can build" — show FINISHED WORK, not proposals.

## Rule 2: Loss Aversion — Mandatory in Every Message

Frame around what the prospect is LOSING RIGHT NOW. Loss aversion is 2x more powerful than gain framing.

**Formula:** WHO + WHEN + WHAT + CONSEQUENCE
- "someone visits your site at 11pm looking for [their service]... no one answers... they find another coach"

**6 loss angles (rotate — never repeat consecutively):**
1. After-hours website visit
2. WhatsApp inquiry during session
3. Weekend research
4. Speed gap (competitor replies faster)
5. Post-content inquiry (interest cools)
6. Referral decay (warm lead goes cold)

Never use negative social proof ("most coaches don't reply fast enough").

## Rule 3: Length — 30-50 Words, Hard Limit 70

Full message visible on lock screen (~5 lines). Shorter captions with video perform BETTER.

## Rule 4: No Links in First Message

Links from unknowns trigger spam filters. Demo links go in FOLLOW-UP only.
Exception: user explicitly says "include link."

## Rule 5: Low-Friction CTA

**Tier 1 (use these — reaction to attached video):**
- PT: "da uma olhada e me fala o que achou" / "ve o video e me diz o que acha"
- EN: "have a look and lmk what you think" / "check it out and let me know"
- ES: "dale un vistazo y dime que te parece"

**Tier 2 (acceptable — opinion):** "faz sentido?" / "what do you think?" / "seria util?"

**Tier 3 (AVOID):** scheduling asks, calendar checks, "can I send it?" (video is already attached).

## Rule 6: Signal-Anchored Specificity

Reference at least ONE specific detail from THEIR business (program name, service, method, URL). Not their niche — THEIR business.

| Never say | Say instead |
|-----------|-------------|
| "AI solution" | "um assistente que ja conhece seus servicos de [coaching executivo]" |
| "automate your business" | "agenda sessoes de [terapia holistica] sozinho" |
| "increase leads" | "responde quem chega no seu site as 23h procurando [mentoria]" |
| "automation" | "funciona 24h" / "responde sozinho" / "agenda automaticamente" |

## Rule 7: Casual Tone — 5th-7th Grade Reading Level

Write like a busy professional texting between calls. No formal greetings, no marketing language, max 1 emoji.

## Rule 8: Two Variants (With/Without Demo Link)

**Both variants** mention BOTH site AND WhatsApp and include the attached video.

| | Variant A (demo_link exists) | Variant B (no demo_link) |
|--|---|---|
| CTA | Reaction-based ("have a look and lmk") | Offer-based ("can send you a link to test") |
| Follow-up | References video + includes demo link | References video + offers live test |

## Rule 9: Anti-AI Detection

**BANNED:** em dashes, semicolons, "leverage/streamline/optimize/seamless/empower/delve/synergy/transformative", "I hope this finds you well", "I'd be happy to", "Great question!", "Furthermore/Moreover", Rule of Three pattern.

**USE:** contractions, casual words ("so", "honestly"), sentence fragments, imperfect phrasing.

## Rule 10: Product Naming — Only "AI assistant" / "assistente AI"

**BANNED:** chatbot, bot, automation, software, tool, system, platform, GPT, demo, solution, auto-reply.

Frame as OUTCOMES: "responde quem chega no site as 23h" / "agenda sessoes direto no calendario".

## Rule 11: "I" vs "We"

"I" for personal actions (I built, I noticed). "We" only for team references (an AI assistant we set up). Never "We offer" / "We at [Company]" / "Our team."

## Rule 12: Caption Framing

The caption creates CONTEXT for the video. Don't over-explain what the demo does (the video shows it). Frame the PROBLEM. Reference the video naturally ("montei isso pro seu site").

## Rule 13: Anti-Fingerprinting

See `references/anti-fingerprinting.md` for full details. Summary:
- **13a:** Rotate element order (X/Y/Z) — never repeat consecutively
- **13b:** Vary sentence rhythm (long-short, short-build, fragment-heavy)
- **13c:** Vary architecture, loss angle, demo detail, opening, CTA
- **13d:** Rotate pain angle and lost-lead pronoun

---

## Follow-Up Rules

Sent ONLY to non-responders after 3-4 days. References the video already sent.

**Variant A** (demo link exists): video reference + demo link + soft question
**Variant B** (no demo link): video reference + offer live test + soft question
**Variant C** (7 days, high-value only): breakup message — "the AI assistant is still live, if not useful I'll take it down, just let me know"

---

## Quality Checklist

**Format:** 30-50 words (70 max) | Tier 1/2 CTA | no links | max 1 emoji | no formal greeting | no company name

**Personalization:** "already built for you" trigger | signal-anchored detail | specific loss scenario | mentions both site AND WhatsApp | CTA matches variant

**Anti-spam:** no spam words | no banned product terms | only "AI assistant" | no pricing | no ALL CAPS | no AI-tell words

**Anti-fingerprinting:** different architecture, loss angle, element order, rhythm, CTA from previous message in batch
